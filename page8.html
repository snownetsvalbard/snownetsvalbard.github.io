SnowNet

A SIOS network project to foster snow research across the Svalbard archipelago. 

Project Aim

The aim is to facilitate multidisciplinary research around snow research stations in Svalbard from black carbon and pollution monitoring, snow ecology, reindeer populations and climate feedback. A clear emphasis is on supporting Early Career Researcher part of the SIOS consortium.
