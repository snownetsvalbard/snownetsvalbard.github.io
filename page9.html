Svalbard Integrated Arctic Earth Observing System

An international observing system for long-term measurements in and around the Norwegian archipelago of Svalbard addressing Earth System Science questions

Link

SIOS backs SnowNet 

SIOS supports and catalyse research in Svalbard including the interdisciplinary SnowNet network for snow researchers. The results are in developing integrated science projects for monitoring snow and snow content for more than 10 years. SIOS is and provides the plateform to exchange on snow data needs with researchers studying atmosphere, ecology, remote sensing, sea ice and glaciology.

SnowNet Contribution to SIOS

SIOS Core Data 

SnowNet delivers essential snow data to the SIOS research community and is at the forefront of providing real time snow measurements. Snow Net also collects  unique datasets on ecology and chemical composition to study in the context of climate change and air pollution.

SESS Report 2019

SIOS publishes the State of Environmental Science in Svalbard (SESS) every year. The report, about the state of the environment in and around Svalbard, is an important tool to convey knowledge to stakeholders and the public.
